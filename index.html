Visit San Antonio

Experience the rich history and lively culture of San Antonio, Texas, home to the iconic Alamo and the picturesque River Walk.

Fun activities to do while in San Antonio

Take a stroll in The Alamo

Visit this historic mission and battle site, a symbol of Texan independence.

See all the animals at the San Antonio Zoo

Discover a diverse array of animals at one of the country's best zoos.

Explore the Natural Bridge Caverns

Stunning underground caves featuring impressive rock formations and adventure tours.

San Antonio food that you MUST try

Mi Tierra Café y Panadería

An iconic Tex-Mex restaurant and bakery known for its vibrant atmosphere and authentic dishes, open 24/7.

Boudro's Texas Bistro

A River Walk favorite offering delectable Texas cuisine with a Southwestern flair, famous for its tableside guacamole.

Rosario's Mexican Café y Cantina

A beloved local spot offering contemporary Mexican dishes and vibrant margaritas in a lively atmosphere.
